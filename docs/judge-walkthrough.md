# Project Phoenix Judge Walkthrough

Maximum runtime: 3 minutes.

## 0:00-0:20 - Open

Click: open `http://localhost:3000`.

Say: "This merge request looks safe: approved review, green pipeline, seven changed files. Phoenix asks a different question: what future does this software decision create?"

Orbit point: "The demo uses synthetic GitLab Orbit context: MR, teams, pipelines, vulnerabilities, incidents, work items, deployments, and objectives."

## 0:20-0:50 - Orbit Becomes The Twin

Click: `Ingest Orbit Context`.

Say: "Phoenix turns Orbit into a living software future twin. Ownership, incidents, vulnerabilities, work items, deployment topology, and business objectives are connected."

Point at: Enterprise Twin View and Orbit Signals Used.

Say: "This is why Phoenix is not an AI code reviewer. The reasoning depends on Orbit context outside the diff."

## 0:50-1:25 - Simulate Futures

Click: `Run Future Simulation`.

Say during the reveal: "Phoenix is fusing the ownership graph, vulnerability graph, incident memory, work item dependencies, deployment topology, and business objectives."

After reveal: "It found one hidden future."

Point at: Future Timeline.

Say: "The green pipeline is only the current state. Phoenix simulates what happens over Day +1, Day +7, and Day +30."

## 1:25-2:10 - Black Swan

Point at: Black Swan Radar.

Say: "The average future was manageable. The dangerous future was a 4.8% tail event: stale mobile clients, JWT dependency drift, and the Enterprise SSO launch combine into customer login failures."

Point at: causal chain.

Say: "Every step traces back to Orbit: merge request, ownership, work items, vulnerability data, deployment topology, historical incidents, and business objectives."

## 2:10-2:45 - Agents Mitigate

Click: `Execute Mitigation Agents`.

Say: "Phoenix does not stop at advice. It coordinates the mitigation: security review, contract validation, JWT patch work item, MR comment, and canary rollout gate."

Point at: risk collapse.

Say: "The blast radius collapses from 71 to 38 because mitigation actions dampen the risk path."

## 2:45-3:00 - Close

Point at: Before / After.

Say: "Before Phoenix: green pipeline, hidden risk, no coordination. After Phoenix: black swan found, validations triggered, security review requested, and risk reduced."

Final line: "Phoenix is the system that used GitLab Orbit to discover a future incident hiding behind a green pipeline."

## Judge Mode

Open `http://localhost:3000?judge=true` for autoplay. It automatically ingests Orbit context, runs the Orbit reveal, discovers the black swan, and executes mitigation over a 90-second cinematic sequence.
